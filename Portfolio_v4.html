EMPLOYEE RELATIONS
Case Routing —

IR & ER ROUTING TOOL

Multi-jurisdiction employee relations routing across UK & India

A decision-support tool built to guide HR professionals through employee relations and industrial relations cases across two distinct legal jurisdictions — the United Kingdom and India. The tool applies the correct statutory framework, process steps, and compliance obligations based on the selected jurisdiction, eliminating the risk of misrouted cases and procedural errors.

Built from real casework experience at Amazon UK (DLS2 & LBA5), the tool maps case type to the correct escalation path, generates a structured case log, and surfaces jurisdiction-specific obligations — from ACAS Early Conciliation in the UK to ICC referral timelines under India's POSH Act.

DESIGNED FROM PRACTICE, BUILT WITH AI — The jurisdictional logic, escalation paths, and statutory frameworks came from real casework. AI was used to bring it into an interactive format.

  • Step-by-step process with statutory timelines
  • ACAS Early Conciliation flag for UK tribunal-bound cases
  • ICC referral logic for POSH Act complaints in India
  • Documentation prompts and case log generation

Workflow —

FLEXIBLE WORK TRACKER

Statutory compliance & workflow automation in Excel

Built while supporting a 2,860-associate multi-site fulfilment operation across two sites. A VBA macro pulled employee data directly from the FCLM system the moment a manager typed in an Employee ID — eliminating manual entry errors and standardising the end-to-end flexible working process across the operation.

The tracker monitored the 92-day statutory deadline under the Employment Relations (Flexible Working) Act 2023 on every case, with a CVD-safe summary dashboard giving 20+ line managers and SLT site-wide visibility without ever exposing personal data.

DESIGNED FROM PRACTICE, BUILT WITH AI — The compliance logic, deadline architecture, and escalation structure came from real casework. AI was used to bring it into an interactive format.

L & D / MANAGEMENT
Enablement —

LEADERSHIP TOOLKIT

Research-grounded leadership framework for a high-growth remote-first organisation

Designed and delivered end-to-end at Vosyn — a fast-scaling AI/tech startup — where rapid intern turnover and frequent leadership transitions were creating operational strain and reactive bottlenecks in the People & Culture team. There was no centralised resource to guide consistent, values-aligned leadership or documented HR procedures for team leads to follow independently.

Research was conducted across 18 managers and team leads using structured focus groups, one-to-one interviews, and a competency survey. Findings were analysed through leadership style quantification and radar competency mapping — producing a bespoke three-domain model and a full toolkit launched to all team leads, deputy team leads, and pod captains across the organisation.

What this covers

  • Leadership style analysis across 18 managers — agile, interpersonal, and strategic dimensions quantified
  • Radar competency mapping across five dimensions with scored insights
  • Three-domain leadership model: Team Management · Interpersonal · Strategic
  • Values-to-behaviours translation — abstract principles made observable and actionable
  • Six standard HR procedures documented end-to-end for manager self-service
  • Working checklists and a governance SOP for quarterly toolkit review
